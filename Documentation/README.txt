Controls:
General
Q - Quit
Right Click - Next Room
Left Click - Previous Room
WASD - Move
1-7 - Use item
Z/N - Attack w/ sword

Notes:
When switching rooms, sometimes Link can teleport all over if he spawns on a block that he cannot walk on.
This is only because his position is the same from room-to-room, this will not be an issue in the final game when he spawns in at the door.
